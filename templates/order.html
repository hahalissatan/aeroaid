1. General Preparation

Step 1: Clear Landing Zone - Ensure a 2m x 2m flat space is clear.

Step 2: Stay with Patient - Do not leave the victim alone.

Step 3: Spot the Drone - Look for the flashing Green LED in the sky.

2. Landing Safety

Step 1: Stand Back - The drone will land automatically. Keep 1 meter clear.

Step 2: Wait for Red LED - When the LED turns Solid Red and the propellers stop, it is safe to touch.

Step 3: Unstrap Kit - Carefully remove the medical aid from the landing legs.
